## Project Overview

This project analyzes how emerging technologies can transform Nanyang Polytechnic's operations, teaching methodologies, and administrative processes. Each team member investigates a specific frontier technology — AI, Immersive Media, Blockchain, and IoT — and evaluates how it can address current challenges, improve the value chain, and create new growth opportunities within Singapore's education sector.

## Proposal Structure

The proposal (`proposal.md`) follows a formal structure aligned with the IT2392 assessment requirements:

1. **Title Page** — Module, team members, submission details
2. **Table of Contents** — Full navigable contents listing
3. **List of Illustrations** — 6 figures and 14 tables
4. **Executive Summary** — Key findings and recommendations
5. **Introduction** — Context, business goals, and scope
6. **Organization Overview** — NYP background, mission, value chain
7. **Current Problems and Challenges** — Six challenges linked to business goals
8. **Market Size and Potential Revenue** — EdTech market analysis and revenue projections
9. **Emerging Technology Analysis** — Four in-depth technology sections (AI, VR/AR, Blockchain, IoT)
10. **Impact on the Digital Economy** — Combined impact, 5-year ROI, adoption roadmap
11. **Conclusion** — Recommendations and phased strategy
12. **References** — 15 cited sources
